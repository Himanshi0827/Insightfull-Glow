Skin Assessment Guide

Oily Skin

If your skin feels consistently oily throughout the day and you notice a shine, you likely have oily skin.

Combination Skin

If you notice oiliness mainly in the T-zone (forehead, nose, chin) but the rest of your face feels normal or dry, you likely have combination skin.



Normal Skin

If your skin feels comfortable throughout the day with minimal oiliness or dryness, you likely have normal skin.

Dry Skin

If your skin feels tight, dry, or rough throughout the day, especially after cleansing, you likely have dry skin.

Skincare Tips
Cleanse your face twice daily to remove dirt and impurities.
Always use sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher when going outside.
Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water to keep your skin moisturized.
Use a gentle exfoliator 2-3 times a week to remove dead skin cells.
Apply a moisturizer after cleansing to keep your skin soft and supple.
Avoid touching your face to prevent the spread of bacteria.
